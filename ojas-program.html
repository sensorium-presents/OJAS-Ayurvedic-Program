Your Correct Tridoshic Profile

Pitta · Kapha · Vata — All Three Matter

You are tridoshic with Pitta dominant at 50%, Kapha secondary at 27%, Vata tertiary at 23%. All three doshas are addressed simultaneously in every element of this program. Not "Pitta-Kapha type" — tridoshic.

🔥 Pitta (50%) — The Fire

Your sharp mind, athletic intensity, perfectionism. Also your cystic back acne, liver heat, skin inflammation, purple PIH scarring on fair skin. Pitta peaks 10am–2pm and 10pm–2am — both critical windows for training timing and sleep protocol.

🌍 Kapha (27%) — The Structure

Your muscle-building capacity, joint stability, patience. Also the deep cystic, slow-healing quality of your acne and the density that traps Ama. Without Kapha you couldn't hold muscle. Needs daily movement or it stagnates.

🌬️ Vata (23%) — The Wind

Nearly equal to Kapha — significant. Your nervous system, creativity, joint mobility. Also your erratic schedule and variable training timing. When Vata destabilises: Pitta gets hotter, Kapha stagnates more. Grounding Vata is foundational.

⚖️ The Key Equation

Ground Vata → Pitta cools → Kapha moves → skin clears. Your consistent routine, warm oil therapies, regular meals, 10pm sleep, and your rich activity schedule (breathwork 4×/week, Sonic Immersion, fascia flossing, tai chi, hot yoga) all serve Vata first, which creates a cascade that benefits all three doshas simultaneously.

Supplement Guidance

Pre-Workout, ZMA & Adaptogens

✅ ZMA — Keep, Time Correctly

Training nights only. Zinc for acne, Magnesium aspartate for deep sleep during Pitta's 10pm repair window, B6 for hormone regulation. Alternate with Magnesium Glycinate on rest nights. Never with dairy or calcium. Empty stomach 30–60 min after dinner.

✅ Yerba Maté + L-Theanine 220mg + Lion's Mane

Conditionally yes on heavy training days only (Chest/Quads or SHARMS). Max 3× per week. Never after noon. Always after eating. L-Theanine at 220mg counteracts caffeine's Pitta-heating and Vata-scattering effects.

❌ Celsius — Avoid Permanently

High-dose caffeine with zero L-Theanine counterbalance. Spikes liver heat for Pitta, scatters Vata, drives the cortisol spike directly connected to your back acne cycle.

✅ Beyond Brew + L-Theanine + Beetroot

Your ideal daily pre-workout base. Mushroom adaptogens cool Pitta, support immunity, enhance focus. Beetroot provides nitric oxide and vasodilation for Kapha activation without overheating Pitta.
